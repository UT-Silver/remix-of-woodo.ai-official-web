诚实原则：三档证据，红线在先The honesty principle: three evidence tiers, red lines first

这个模型把 final test 时长拆成几个可解释的来源，再用少数渠道锚点校准、外推到未来芯片。它有用，但它不是一套从第一性原理推导出来的物理定律。文件里每一个数字，我都标了它属于哪一档证据——这样你在用它下判断时，知道自己踩的是水泥还是脚手架。 The model decomposes final-test time into a few interpretable drivers, then calibrates to a handful of channel anchors and extrapolates to future chips. It is useful — but it is not a physical law derived from first principles. Every number here is tagged with its evidence tier, so when you lean on it you know whether you're standing on concrete or on scaffolding.

Part 1 · 背景知识Background

你需要的半导体测试背景The semiconductor-test background you need

模型的每一项都对应一个真实的测试工程现象。先把这些现象讲清楚，后面看公式就不是背系数，而是认因果。Every term in the model maps to a real test-engineering phenomenon. Get those straight first, and the formulas later read as cause-and-effect, not memorized coefficients.

1.1 测试在哪一步：CP → FT → burn-in → SLTWhere testing happens: CP → FT → burn-in → SLT

一颗芯片一生要被测很多次。CP（晶圆测试 / wafer sort）在切割前逐 die 测；FT（final test / 终测）在封装后测成品；burn-in（烧机）用高温高压加速早夭失效；SLT（system-level test / 系统级测试）把芯片插到接近真实主板的环境跑 mission-mode 负载。四种测试用三类不同设备、算三本不同的账——这是理解本模型"口径"的第一件事。 A chip is tested many times over its life. CP (wafer sort) probes each die before dicing; FT (final test) tests the packaged part; burn-in uses high voltage/temperature to precipitate early-life failures; SLT (system-level test) drops the chip into a near-real motherboard to run mission-mode workloads. These four use three different classes of equipment and live on three separate accounts — the first thing to grasp about this model's "caliber".

本模型的核心 T_FT 只算 FT 这一步的秒数（含量产初期的多轮 retest）。burn-in（小时级）和 SLT（分钟级）是另外两本账，不进 T_FT——把它们混进来是最常见的口径错误。 The model's core T_FT counts only the FT step (including in-ramp retest rounds). Burn-in (hours) and SLT (minutes) are two separate accounts and do not enter T_FT — conflating them is the single most common caliber error.

1.2 FT 里的主角：scan / ATPG 结构测试The star of FT: scan / ATPG structural test

FT 时间的大头不是"跑程序看它算得对不对"（那是 functional test），而是 structural test / 结构测试：把成千上万个触发器串成 scan chain（扫描链），移入自动生成的测试向量（ATPG），针对制造缺陷模型（stuck-at 固定型、transition 跳变型、bridging 桥接型等）逐一验证"这颗芯片是不是按版图正确造出来的"。结构测试先跑、极快（毫秒级向量）、目标 99%+ 覆盖，抓住绝大多数制造缺陷；functional test 随后补系统行为——量产两者都要。 The bulk of FT time is not "run a program and check the answer" (that's functional test) but structural test: thousands of flip-flops are stitched into scan chains, auto-generated vectors (ATPG) are shifted through them, and each is checked against manufacturing-defect fault models (stuck-at, transition, bridging…) to verify the chip was built to the layout. Structural test runs first, is very fast (millisecond vectors), targets 99%+ coverage and catches most manufacturing defects; functional test follows for system behavior — production needs both. 1.3 为什么时间随晶体管涨：test data volume 与压缩跑步机Why time rises with transistors: test-data volume and the compression treadmill

1.6 Burn-in 与 SLT：浴盆曲线、以及"静默数据损坏"如何改写规则Burn-in & SLT: the bathtub curve, and how "silent data corruption" rewrote the rules

Burn-in 的物理：失效率随寿命呈"浴盆曲线"——早期"婴儿死亡率"段（缺陷件早早失效，风险递减，Weibull 形状参数 k<1）、中段随机失效、末段磨损。Burn-in 就是用应力把婴儿死亡率段提前"逼"出来、筛掉。历史上多数消费芯片良率成熟后就撤掉 burn-in；但 HPC/AI 器件正在把 burn-in 加回来——一颗芯片装进数据中心几小时后失效的代价，远高于多加一道 burn-in（Advantest 的 Davette Berry）。2026 补强:标准层面,JEDEC JESD22-A108G(2022)明文将 burn-in 定位为筛除 infant-mortality 失效的手段;产业层面,KYEC 为 Blackwell 把烧机炉从 600W 升级到 1kW,公开报道的流程为 FT→burn-in→FT→SLT。 Burn-in physics: failure rate over life follows a "bathtub curve" — an early "infant-mortality" region (defective parts fail early, hazard decreasing, Weibull shape k<1), a flat random-failure middle, and a wear-out tail. Burn-in applies stress to force out and screen the infant-mortality region. Most consumer chips historically dropped burn-in once yield matured; but HPC/AI devices are adding it back — a chip failing hours after data-center install costs far more than one more burn-in insertion (Advantest's Davette Berry). 2026 reinforcement: at the standards level, JEDEC JESD22-A108G (2022) codifies burn-in as a screen for infant-mortality failures; at the industry level, KYEC upgraded burn-in chambers from 600W to 1kW for Blackwell, with a publicly reported FT→burn-in→FT→SLT flow. Part 2 · 测算逻辑The calculation logic

三套公式，不是一套Three formulas, not one

理解全局的第一件事：模型故意不用一条定律套所有芯片。不同类芯片的测试物理主导不同，硬套一条公式就是"强行耦合"。The first thing to grasp: the model deliberately refuses one law for all chips. Different chip classes are dominated by different test physics; forcing one formula on all of them would be exactly the "coupling" to avoid.

2.1 口径声明（最重要的一句）The caliber declaration (the one sentence that matters most)

T_FT = 投产期单颗 final test 口径，含轮次。它不含 burn-in（小时级另账）、不含 SLT（分钟级另账）、CP/die-sort 只通过 T_ATE 的乘数进入。你的三锚点（360/1080/1512）就是这个口径。Advantest CEO 的"3-4×Hopper"是全流程数十次插入的合计口径，不能和单次 FT 直接对表——这是最常见的口径混淆。 T_FT = in-ramp single final-test, including retest rounds. It excludes burn-in (hours, separate) and SLT (minutes, separate); CP/die-sort enter only via the T_ATE multiplier. Your three anchors (360/1080/1512) are in this caliber. The Advantest CEO's "3–4× Hopper" is a full-flow, dozens-of-insertions figure and cannot be compared directly to a single FT — the most common caliber confusion. Advantest CEO 3–4× (full-flow)

Part 5 · 红线Red lines

三条不能强行耦合的红线Three "do-not-force-couple" red lines

如果把下面这些当成自洽物理，就是过度耦合。这是整份文件最该记住的部分。Treating any of these as self-consistent physics is over-coupling. This is the part of the document to remember.

① E 和 κ 不是干净的物理量，它们是"残差吸收器"① E and κ are not clean physical quantities — they are "residual absorbers"

模型自己承认"线性 scan 是简化，误差由 E/κ 吸收"。所以 Rubin 的 E=1.45 在做双重工作：一部分是真实的 N3P DFT 改善（scan-over-HSIO 量产化），另一部分只是"线性公式在 336B 晶体管处算过头了，E 把它摁回 1512"。→ 不要讲成"Rubin 有 1.45× 的纯 DFT 效率提升"。若 Rubin 实际 E≈1，336B 会算出 ~2000s，1512 锚点就无法与晶体管数调和——这是全模型最强、也最脆的结构性假设。 The model admits "linear scan is a simplification, the error absorbed by E/κ." So Rubin's E=1.45 does double duty: part is real N3P DFT improvement (scan-over-HSIO in HVM), part is just "the linear formula overshoots at 336B transistors and E pushes it back to 1512." → Do not narrate it as "Rubin has a pure 1.45× DFT gain." If Rubin's real E≈1, 336B computes ~2000s and the 1512 anchor can't reconcile with the transistor count — the model's strongest and most fragile structural assumption.

② 三个锚点是承重墙，其余是脚手架——别把脚手架当地基② The three anchors are load-bearing walls, the rest is scaffolding — don't mistake scaffolding for foundation

k、λ、E 三个系数被三个渠道点钉死；h_hbm、C_fixed、γ、δ 是残差和假设。这意味着模型的绝对值只在你三个锚点的口径下有意义（投产期单颗 FT、含轮次、不含 burn-in/SLT）。→ 正确用法是相对比较与敏感性；换口径去对表（如 CEO"3-4×"是全流程数十次插入）必然打架，那不是模型错，是口径错配。而且所有独立验证点都是 NVIDIA/AMD 同族——模型从未跨族验证，TPU 40-64% 分歧就是物理故事的边界。 k, λ, E are pinned by three channel points; h_hbm, C_fixed, γ, δ are residuals and assumptions. So the model's absolute values are meaningful only in your anchors' caliber (in-ramp single FT, incl. rounds, excl. burn-in/SLT). → Use it for relative comparison and sensitivity; comparing across calibers (the CEO's "3–4×" is full-flow dozens-of-insertions) will always clash — not a model error but a caliber mismatch. And every independent validator is a NVIDIA/AMD same-family chip — the model has never been validated out-of-family; the 40–64% TPU divergences are the physical story's boundary.

③ 需求桥的 φ=0.88 是"反推校准到官方数"，它是循环的③ The bridge's φ=0.88 is reverse-fitted to the official number — it is circular

φ 被调到让 TAM26 落进 Advantest 官方 8.7–9.5 带。→ 那你就不能反过来说"模型独立预测了官方 TAM"——它是被拟合上去的。桥的价值在熊/基准/牛三情景的结构（哪个变量松动会打穿官方带），不在那个中枢点本身。 φ is tuned so TAM26 lands inside Advantest's official 8.7–9.5 band. → You therefore cannot claim "the model independently predicted the official TAM" — it was fitted to it. The bridge's value is in the structure of the bear/base/bull scenarios (which variable, when it moves, breaks the official band), not in the central point itself.

✓ 那模型到底"对"在哪✓ So what is the model actually right about

它把一个模糊直觉（"AI 芯片测试越来越久"）拆成可证伪的分项驱动，每个都能单独跟踪、单独回填。它的相对排序（谁比谁测得久、代际斜率多陡）比任何单点绝对值都稳。用它做敏感性和跨 SKU 比较，把绝对秒数交给渠道去钉——这才是这套引擎的正确打开方式。 It decomposes a fuzzy intuition ("AI chips test longer and longer") into falsifiable per-driver components, each independently trackable and back-fillable. Its relative ordering (which chip tests longer, how steep the generational slope) is far more robust than any single absolute value. Use it for sensitivity and cross-SKU comparison, and let channel checks pin the absolute seconds — that is the right way to hold this engine.
